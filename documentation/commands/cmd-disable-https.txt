~ Name:
~ ~~~~~
~ disable-https -- Disable HTTPS and HTTP/2; keep the keystore for re-enabling later
~
~ Synopsis:
~ ~~~~~~~~~
~ play disable-https [app_path]
~
~ Description:
~ ~~~~~~~~~~~~
~ Comments out the https.port and play.http2.enabled lines in
~ conf/application.conf so the app listens on plain HTTP only after
~ the next play run / play start.
~
~ The keystore at conf/certificate.jks is preserved, as are the
~ keystore.algorithm / keystore.file / keystore.password lines in
~ conf/application.conf. This means a subsequent play enable-https
~ reuses the existing cert and password without regeneration or
~ prompting.
~
~ To permanently remove the keystore, delete conf/certificate.jks
~ manually. The command never deletes it on its own.
~
~ Idempotent: re-running when HTTPS is already disabled is a no-op.
~
~ See also: play enable-https
~
