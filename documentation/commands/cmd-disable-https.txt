~ Name:
~ ~~~~~
~ disable-https -- Disable HTTPS; keep the cert files for re-enabling later
~
~ Synopsis:
~ ~~~~~~~~~
~ play disable-https [app_path]
~
~ Description:
~ ~~~~~~~~~~~~
~ Comments out the https.port line in conf/application.conf so the app
~ listens on plain HTTP only after the next play run / play start. The
~ http.port line is left active.
~
~ The PEM cert+key at certs/host.cert and certs/host.key are preserved,
~ as are the certificate.file and certificate.key.file lines in
~ conf/application.conf. This means a subsequent play enable-https
~ reuses the existing cert without regeneration or prompting.
~
~ To permanently remove the cert+key, delete certs/host.cert and
~ certs/host.key manually. The command never deletes them on its own.
~
~ Idempotent: re-running when HTTPS is already disabled is a no-op.
~
~ See also: play enable-https
~
